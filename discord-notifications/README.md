# Discord Notifications

**Keep your community and team in the loop with real-time server alerts.**

Seamlessly integrate your GameCP servers with Discord. Automatically send status updates, crash reports, and player activity notifications directly to your designated channels. It's the perfect way to keep your staff informed and your community engaged.

![Banner](assets/screenshots/1.png)

## Why you need this

*   **Never miss a crash:** Get instant alerts if a server goes down so you can fix it immediately.
*   **Keep players informed:** Automatically notify your community when servers restart or update.
*   **Monitor activity:** Track server performance and lifecycle events without leaving Discord.

## How it works

Once installed, simply navigate to your game server's extension tab, paste in your Discord Webhook URL, and select the events you want to subscribe to. The extension handles the rest, running silently in the background to monitor your server status.
